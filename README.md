BBC Ticker Ruby Script
======================

Parses the BBC Sport and News tickers that are provided in JSON by the BBC website (UK) and posts them to two Twitter accounts.

Confirmed working in Ruby 1.9.1

*** LOCAL GEMS ***

* bitly (0.7.0)
* crack (0.3.1)
* hashie (1.2.0)
* htmlentities (4.3.1)
* httparty (0.8.3)
* oauth (0.4.6)
* ruby-hmac (0.4.0)
* twitter (2.2.5)
